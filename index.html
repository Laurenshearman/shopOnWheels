FIND US

Opening hours: 8am to 5pm.

Address: 52 NORTH STREET BN2 7GJ

Welcome to our vintage clothing pop-up store on wheels! We are a mobile boutique that specializes in offering a unique and curated selection of high-quality vintage clothing items. Our van has been retrofitted to serve as a mobile boutique, complete with clothing racks, a dressing room, and a checkout area. We carefully source our inventory from vintage wholesalers, estate sales, garage sales, and flea markets to ensure that each piece is unique and has a story to tell.

Our goal is to provide a convenient and unique shopping experience for our customers by bringing our store directly to them. We park our van in high-traffic areas, such as markets, festivals, or fairs, and also offer private appointments for customers who want a more personalized shopping experience. We also offer personalized styling advice and host interactive workshops or events to create a sense of community among our customers. Follow us on social media to stay up-to-date on our upcoming events and inventory. Thank you for visiting our website and we can't wait to see you at our next pop-up event!
